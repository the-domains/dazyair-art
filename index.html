DAZYAIR ART

Part of a series of graphic work I started when i was visiting home. It started with my mom and her husband (ED Lucina 1956-2012) on a trip home to Great Falls Montana just after the 100 year celebration. 100 Buffalo lined the streets of Great Falls during the celebration and now they are scattered around the town. I was inspired so I grabbed my camera and we went on a Buffalo hunt.

This Land is your land.
